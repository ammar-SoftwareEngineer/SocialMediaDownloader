Download videos from social media

1. Copy the URL

Open the which social page with a video that you want to save, copy its URL and go back to SMD.github

2. Paste the URL onto the input field

Paste the URL of the into the input field of the top of the page and click on the right side the button to run the downloading process.

3. Choose one of the downloads

If you want to download a video or convert it to music, or translate it

4. DOWNLODE MP4 OR MP3

We have high quality video or music downloads in the selection download mp4 or mp3

5. CONVERT TO ADUIO

The video can be converted to music in high quality

6. TRANSLATE VIDEO

We have video downloads and subtitles in multiple languages in the choice of download mp4 and subtitles and not just one choice
